Office Team

Dr. Susan C. King MD
Board Certified Family Doctor

Dr King moved to Texas from Canada in 2000 after finishing her residency in Family medicine in 1999 at Memorial University of Newfoundland. She started her practice at 12 Oaks Medical Center in Austin next door to her Father Dr. David King and good friend Dr. Humberto Enriquez. She has an undergrad in Electrical Engineering from The University of Toronto.

Dr. King has an interest in preventative health and metabolic medicine , women’s health and Hormone therapy for men and women. She also has experience in treating a wide range of issues including anxiety and depression, diabetes, hypertension, thyroid disorders and skin disorders.

Dr. King enjoys exercise, being outdoors running and hiking as well as strength training and pilates. In her spare time she enjoys baking, spending time and traveling with her son and daughter.
