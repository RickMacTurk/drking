After Hours Calls

After hours physician advice is available on a 24 hour basis, 7 days a week for urgent medical problems that cannot wait until the next business day. Patients using the after hours service must be a current patient and seen within the last year. The after hours pager is not for refills, appointments or non urgent matters.
To contact the on-call doctor please call the pager number 512-622-0048 and leave a message. Your call will be returned by the on call physician within 60 minutes. Prescription requests for new prescriptions, antibiotics or controlled substances will not be filled by the physician on call. Dr. King shares on-call duties with Dr. Gevin Wilhelm on a rotating weekly basis.

Emergencies

If you have an emergency, DO NOT call the pager CALL 911 IMMEDIATELY!
