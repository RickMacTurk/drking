After Hours Calls

An after hours physician is available after 5pm on weekdays and 24 hours on weekends. The on-call physician is available for urgent medical problems that cannot wait until the next business day. Patients using the after hours service must be a current patient and seen within the last 6 months. The after hours pager is not for refills, new prescriptions, antibiotics, controlled substances or appointment requests.
To contact the on-call doctor please call the pager number 512-622-0048 and leave a message. Your call will be returned by the on call physician within 60 minutes. Prescription requests for new prescriptions, antibiotics or controlled substances will not be filled by the physician on call.

Emergencies

If you have an emergency, DO NOT call the pager CALL 911 IMMEDIATELY!
